This directory contains data files analyzed in:

Barba-Montoya et al. (2024). Integrating phylogenies with chronology to assemble the Tree of Life. https://doi.org/...

Two directories, one for simulated data, and one for empirical data:

	empirical_data -> mammal timetrees from Álvarez-Carretero et al. (2022), and simiiform timetrees from TimeTree Database (Kumar et al. 2022).
	simulated_data -> simulated MSAs and timetree collections from Barba-Montoya et al. (2024).
